# Reset Reason

The repository accumulated too much complexity across runtime code, launchers, discovery experiments, publishing adapters, CI checks, local service assumptions, and historical roadmap documents.

Twikit, Nitter, and browser-based scout experiments did not provide a reliable enough foundation for the product. They failed for different reasons, and continuing to patch them would likely add more fragility instead of clarifying the product.

The decision was to stop patching the old implementation, preserve the important knowledge, and wipe the active runtime.

This archive keeps the useful context for a future rebuild.

No MVP is defined yet.
