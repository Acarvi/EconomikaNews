# Product Intent Before Reset

This is historical intent, not final product definition.

The previous idea was to automate political/economic content creation:

- detect viral tweets from specific configured accounts;
- calculate virality;
- let the user choose candidate topics;
- generate pieces, reels, or shorts;
- publish to Instagram and YouTube.

This intent should be revisited with the client and a senior engineer before rebuilding. It should not be treated as binding requirements.
